ABOUT ME

Click Here to View & Download the Biodata and HD Photos

Shri Kiran Godkhindi, a talented tabla player of this generation, hails from a family of musicians. His father, Pt. Venkatesh Godkhindi, a renowned flautist and vocalist, initiated his training at the age of three. Kiran received formal training in tabla from various gurus, such as Pt. Prabhakar Chari of Goa, Pt. NandaKumar, a disciple of the legendary tabla player Ustaad Sheikh Dawood of Hyderabad, Pt. Lalgiriji Goswami, Ustaad Abdullah of Delhi, and Pt. Raghunath Nakod of Dharwad.

Photos

He holds a diploma in computer engineering, and has been busy working from his home studio, and producing a variety of music for different purposes like jingles, title songs, dramas, re-recordings for T.V. serials and films, music for meditation, voice over, and lot more.

ACHIEVEMENTS

A 30-year veteran in the music industry, he has shared the stage with his father, Pt. Venkatesh Godkhindi, and his younger brother, Pt. Pravin Godkhindi. Has accompanied various artists in prestigious concerts around the country .

This highly skilled percussionist is a well-known figure in radio and television. He has traveled around the world performing as the lead percussionist of "KRISHNA," a fusion band led by Pravin Godkhindi. Additionally, he is a member of the "LAYA LAHARI" and "LAYA LAVANYA" percussion ensembles, both led by the renowned mridangam artist Vidwan Anoor Ananthkrishna Sharma.
